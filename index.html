    Shri Gnanambica Degree College is located in Madanapalle and provides quality higher education. The college offers various undergraduate courses with experienced faculty and modern facilities. It focuses on student development, innovation, and career guidance. The institution encourages discipline, skills, and knowledge among students. It also supports extracurricular activities and research programs.

Correspondent – R. GaruPrasad sir

    R. Garu Prasad is a visionary leader committed to providing quality education and overall development of students. He strongly believes that education should not only focus on academics but also on building character, values, and leadership qualities. He continuously works towards improving the infrastructure of the college by introducing modern facilities and advanced learning resources. His dedication has helped the institution grow steadily and gain a good reputation in the field of higher education. He encourages both students and faculty to adopt innovative ideas and modern teaching methods. Under his guidance, the college has created a positive learning environment that supports creativity and skill development. He also focuses on providing better career opportunities and industry exposure for students. His leadership motivates everyone in the institution to strive for excellence and achieve their goals. Through his vision and hard work, many students have successfully built their careers in different fields. He is dedicated to taking the college to greater heights and making it a center of excellence in education.

Principal – Sri Ramadevi medam

    Sri Ramadevi is a highly experienced academic leader with more than 20 years of teaching experience in the field of education. She is known for her dedication, discipline, and commitment towards student success and academic excellence. She always encourages students to focus on knowledge, skill development, and personal growth. Under her leadership, the college has achieved good academic standards and a strong reputation. She supports modern teaching methods and motivates faculty members to adopt innovative approaches in education. Her guidance helps students build confidence and achieve their career goals. She believes that education plays a key role in shaping a better future for individuals and society. She also promotes values, ethics, and responsibility among students. Many students have achieved success in different fields under her mentorship. Her vision is to create a positive and inspiring learning environment for every student.
